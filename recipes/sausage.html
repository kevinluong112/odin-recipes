Spicy Sausage Balls

Description

these sausage balls are good as Bisquick sausage balls, but without the carbs and gluten

they are keto friendly! Eat them fresh oit of the oven or refrigerate or freeze for later use

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk spicy pork sausage
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese, ore more taste
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  • 1 large egg

Step

  1. preheat the oven to 325 degrees
  2. Combine sausage, Cheddar cheese, almond flour, coconut flour
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through 23 to 28 minutes and remove sausage balls about 5 minutes
