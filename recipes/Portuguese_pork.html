Portuguese Pork with Red Peppers

Description

One of my favorite flavor combinations ever! this Pork dish combines the strength of garlic with the sweetness of red pepper balanced by a tangy lemon garnish

Ingredients

  • 4 large cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 teaspoons coarse salt
  • 2 tablespoons olives oil

Steps

  1. With a large mortar and pestle, mash the garlic, coarse slat, 1 tablespoon olive
  2. With a mallet, flatten the pork medallions
  3. Heat the remaining oil in a large skillet over high heat
  4. Place red peppers into the skillet and saute 2 to 5 minutes
  5. slice 1 and 1/2 lemons into thin rounds. Transfer the pork and pepper mixture to a serving plate
