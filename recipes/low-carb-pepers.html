Low-Carb stuffed peppers

Description

Enjoy these traditional savory stuffed peppers without all the extra carbohydrates

You can use any kind of ground sausage hot or mild, your choice

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 6 Roma tomatoes, chopped, divided

Steps

  1. Coat a casserole dish with cooking spray
  2. Cook ground sirloin and sausage in a skillet over medium-low heat until browned
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  4. Fill bell peppers with meat mixture, alternating with layers of feta cheese and Parmesan cheese. Place stuffed bell peppers side by side in the prepared casserole dish; and remaining tomatoes around and under the peppers so they cook up and into the peppers
